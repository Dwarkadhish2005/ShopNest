SHIPPING POLICY — ShopNest
Last Updated: April 2026
=============================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
ShopNest is committed to delivering your orders quickly, safely, and affordably.
This Shipping Policy explains how we process, ship, and deliver your orders,
including estimated delivery times, shipping costs, and what to do if something
goes wrong.

---------------------------------------------------------------
1. ORDER PROCESSING TIME
---------------------------------------------------------------
- Orders are processed Monday through Saturday, excluding public holidays.
- Orders placed before 12:00 PM (noon) IST on a business day are typically
  processed the same day.
- Orders placed after 12:00 PM IST or on weekends/holidays are processed the
  next business day.
- Processing time: 1-2 business days before shipment.
- You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once
  your order has been dispatched.

Note: During peak seasons (sale events, festivals, holidays), processing times
may increase by 1-3 business days. We appreciate your patience.

---------------------------------------------------------------
2. SHIPPING METHODS AND ESTIMATED DELIVERY TIMES
---------------------------------------------------------------
We offer the following shipping options at checkout:

a) Standard Shipping (Free on orders above Rs. 499 / $10):
   - Estimated delivery: 5-7 business days (domestic)
   - Carrier: India Post / DTDC / Delhivery (India); USPS / FedEx Ground (US)

b) Express Shipping:
   - Estimated delivery: 2-3 business days
   - Cost: Rs. 149 / $9.99 flat rate
   - Carrier: Blue Dart / FedEx / Ekart Logistics

c) Same-Day Delivery (select cities only):
   - Available in: Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune
   - Order must be placed before 10:00 AM IST
   - Cost: Rs. 249 flat rate
   - Estimated delivery: By 9:00 PM on the same day

d) International Shipping:
   - Available to 50+ countries
   - Estimated delivery: 10-20 business days
   - Cost: Calculated at checkout based on destination and weight
   - Carrier: DHL / FedEx International / EMS

Note: Delivery times are estimates and not guarantees. Delays may occur due to
customs, weather, or carrier disruptions.

---------------------------------------------------------------
3. SHIPPING COSTS
---------------------------------------------------------------
Domestic (India):
- Orders below Rs. 499:       Rs. 49 flat shipping fee
- Orders Rs. 499 and above:   FREE standard shipping
- Express shipping:           Rs. 149 (any order value)
- Same-day delivery:          Rs. 249 (select cities only)

International:
- Shipping cost is calculated at checkout.
- Import duties, customs fees, and taxes are NOT included in the product price
  or shipping fee and are the responsibility of the buyer.

---------------------------------------------------------------
4. SHIPMENT TRACKING
---------------------------------------------------------------
- Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email
  and SMS.
- You can track your order at: www.shopnest.com/track-order
- Alternatively, enter your tracking number directly on the carrier's website.
- Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to update after dispatch.

If your tracking information has not updated for more than 3 business days,
please contact us at support@shopnest.com.

---------------------------------------------------------------
5. DELIVERY ATTEMPTS AND UNDELIVERED PACKAGES
---------------------------------------------------------------
- Our shipping partners will attempt delivery up to 3 times.
- If delivery fails after 3 attempts, the package will be returned to our
  warehouse.
- In the event of a return due to failed delivery:
  * We will contact you within 2 business days.
  * You may request a re-shipment (additional shipping charges apply) or
    a refund of the product cost (minus original shipping fees).

- ShopNest is not responsible for packages that are undeliverable due to:
  * Incorrect address provided by the customer
  * Recipient not available after repeated attempts
  * Refusal of delivery

---------------------------------------------------------------
6. WRONG OR INCOMPLETE ADDRESS
---------------------------------------------------------------
- Please double-check your shipping address before placing an order.
- Once an order is dispatched, we cannot change the delivery address.
- If you notice an error in your address, contact us immediately at
  support@shopnest.com before the order is dispatched.
- ShopNest is not liable for lost packages due to incorrect addresses provided
  by the customer.

---------------------------------------------------------------
7. LOST OR STOLEN PACKAGES
---------------------------------------------------------------
If your order shows as "Delivered" but you have not received it:

Step 1: Wait 24 hours — packages are sometimes marked delivered prematurely.
Step 2: Check with neighbors, building reception, or safe drop locations.
Step 3: Contact us at support@shopnest.com with your order number.

We will file a claim with the carrier and investigate the issue. If the package
is confirmed lost, we will send a replacement or issue a full refund.

Note: Claims for lost packages must be filed within 7 days of the expected
delivery date.

---------------------------------------------------------------
8. DAMAGED PACKAGES
---------------------------------------------------------------
If your package arrives visibly damaged:

- Do NOT discard the packaging — it may be needed for the carrier's insurance claim.
- Take photos/videos of the damaged packaging and product.
- Report the damage within 48 hours of delivery to support@shopnest.com.
- We will arrange a replacement or full refund including return shipping.

---------------------------------------------------------------
9. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING — SPECIAL NOTES
---------------------------------------------------------------
- International orders may be subject to customs inspection and delays.
- Import duties, taxes, and customs fees are the buyer's responsibility and
  are NOT collected by ShopNest at checkout.
- ShopNest is not responsible for delays caused by customs or government
  agencies in the destination country.
- Some products (e.g., electronics, lithium batteries, liquids) may be
  restricted for international shipping based on destination country regulations.
- If your order is confiscated by customs, ShopNest is not liable for
  replacement or refund.

---------------------------------------------------------------
10. SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------
We currently do not ship to:
- P.O. Boxes (for express and same-day delivery only)
- Military APO/FPO addresses (international)
- Certain remote areas where carrier services are unavailable

If your area is not serviceable, you will be notified at checkout.
