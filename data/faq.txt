FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ) — ShopNest
Last Updated: April 2026
=============================================================

This document contains answers to the most common customer questions.
It is organized by category to help the AI support agent quickly retrieve
and respond to customer queries.

=============================================================
SECTION A: ORDERS & PURCHASING
=============================================================

Q1: How do I place an order on ShopNest?
A: To place an order, browse our website and add items to your cart. When ready,
   click "Checkout", enter your shipping address, choose a shipping method, and
   complete payment. You will receive an order confirmation email immediately.

Q2: Can I order without creating an account?
A: Yes. ShopNest supports Guest Checkout. However, creating an account lets you
   track orders, save addresses, earn loyalty points, and view order history.

Q3: How do I know if my order was placed successfully?
A: You will receive a confirmation email with your Order ID within 5-10 minutes
   of placing your order. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

Q4: Can I make changes to my order after placing it?
A: You can modify your order (change size, color, or address) only if it has not
   been dispatched yet. Contact us immediately at support@shopnest.com or via
   live chat. Once dispatched, changes are not possible.

Q5: Can I add items to an existing order?
A: No. Once an order is placed, you cannot add items to it. Please place a
   separate order for any additional items.

Q6: Why was my order not placed even though payment was deducted?
A: This can happen due to a technical error. The deducted amount will be
   automatically refunded within 5-7 business days. Contact support@shopnest.com
   with your transaction reference number if it is not refunded.

Q7: Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders?
A: Yes. For bulk orders above 50 units, contact our B2B team at
   wholesale@shopnest.com for special pricing and dedicated support.

=============================================================
SECTION B: PAYMENTS
=============================================================

Q8: What payment methods do you accept?
A: ShopNest accepts the following payment methods:
   - Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay, Amex)
   - UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
   - Net Banking (all major banks)
   - ShopNest Wallet
   - EMI (on orders above Rs. 3000, select banks)
   - Cash on Delivery (COD) — available on orders below Rs. 5000 in select locations
   - PayPal (international orders)

Q9: Is it safe to use my card on ShopNest?
A: Yes. ShopNest uses 256-bit SSL encryption and is PCI-DSS compliant. We do not
   store your card details on our servers.

Q10: Why was my payment declined?
A: Possible reasons include: insufficient funds, incorrect card details, bank
   security block, or expired card. Try another payment method or contact your
   bank. If the problem persists, contact support@shopnest.com.

Q11: Can I pay using multiple payment methods for a single order?
A: You can use a combination of ShopNest Wallet balance + one other payment method.
   Splitting between two cards or UPI and a card is not supported at this time.

Q12: How does Cash on Delivery (COD) work?
A: COD is available for eligible orders. Pay the exact amount to the delivery
   person at the time of delivery. COD orders cannot be partially paid — have
   the exact amount ready.

Q13: How do I apply a coupon or promo code?
A: At checkout, enter your coupon code in the "Promo Code" field and click "Apply".
   Only one promo code can be used per order. Codes cannot be combined with
   other offers unless stated otherwise.

Q14: Do promo codes have an expiry date?
A: Yes. Each promo code has a validity period displayed in the promotion. Expired
   codes cannot be applied even if they appear in your email history.

=============================================================
SECTION C: SHIPPING & DELIVERY
=============================================================

Q15: How long does delivery take?
A: Estimated delivery times:
   - Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
   - Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
   - Same-Day Delivery (select cities): By 9 PM same day (order before 10 AM IST)
   - International: 10-20 business days

Q16: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email
   and SMS. Track your order at www.shopnest.com/track-order or on the
   carrier's website.

Q17: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
A: Address changes are possible only before dispatch. Contact support@shopnest.com
   immediately. Once dispatched, we cannot change the delivery address.

Q18: What happens if I miss my delivery?
A: The carrier will attempt delivery up to 3 times. After 3 failed attempts, the
   package is returned to our warehouse and you will be contacted for further action.

Q19: Do you deliver on Sundays and holidays?
A: Standard and Express shipping partners may deliver on Sundays in metro cities.
   Same-day delivery does not operate on national holidays. Check carrier
   information in your tracking details.

Q20: I live in a remote area. Do you deliver there?
A: We deliver to most serviceable pin codes. Enter your pin code at checkout to
   check availability. If your area is not serviceable, you will be notified.

Q21: My tracking shows "Delivered" but I haven't received the package. What do I do?
A: Wait 24 hours as packages are sometimes scanned early. Check with neighbors
   or building security. If still not found, contact support@shopnest.com within
   7 days of the expected delivery date.

SECTION D: RETURNS & REFUNDS
=============================================================

Q22: What is ShopNest's return policy?
A: Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery if they are unused, in
   original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase. See our full
   Refund Policy for details.

Q23: How do I initiate a return?
A: Log into your account, go to "My Orders", select the item, and click "Return
   Item". Fill in the reason and submit. You'll receive an RMA number and return
   instructions within 2 business days.

Q24: How long does it take to get a refund?
A: After we receive and inspect the returned item (3-5 business days), the
   refund is credited to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.
   Total time: approximately 5-15 business days from return receipt.

Q25: Can I return a sale or discounted item?
A: Items bought during regular sales can be returned if they meet return
   conditions. Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" at the time of
   purchase are not eligible for return.

Q26: What if I received a wrong or defective item?
A: Report it within 48 hours of delivery by emailing support@shopnest.com with
   photos and your order number. We will send a replacement or issue a full
   refund at no cost to you.

Q27: Do I have to pay for return shipping?
A: Only if the return is due to change of mind. If the return is due to our error
   (wrong item, damage, defect), we provide a free prepaid return label.

Q28: Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?
A: Yes. During the return request, select "Exchange" and choose the new size or
   color. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

=============================================================
SECTION E: CANCELLATIONS
=============================================================

Q29: How do I cancel my order?
A: Go to "My Orders" on our website, select the order, and click "Cancel Order".
   Cancellation is instant before dispatch. If the order is already dispatched,
   you may refuse delivery for a refund (minus return shipping charges).

Q30: Can I cancel only one item in a multi-item order?
A: Yes. Go to "My Orders", select the order, and click "Cancel Item" next to
   the specific product. This is possible only before that item is dispatched.

Q31: I cancelled my order. When will I get my refund?
A: Cancellations before dispatch: 5-7 business days.
   Refused delivery (post-dispatch): 7-10 business days after we receive
   the returned package.

Q32: Can ShopNest cancel my order?
A: Yes, in rare cases — such as stock unavailability, payment issues, or
   pricing errors. We will notify you by email and issue a full refund.

SECTION F: ACCOUNT & PROFILE
=============================================================

Q33: How do I create a ShopNest account?
A: Click "Sign Up" on our website, enter your name, email, and password.
   You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account.

Q34: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address
   and we'll send a password reset link valid for 30 minutes.

Q35: How do I update my phone number or email address?
A: Log in, go to "My Account" > "Profile Settings" and update your details.
   Changing your email requires OTP verification on both old and new email.

Q36: How do I delete my ShopNest account?
A: To delete your account, email support@shopnest.com with your registered
   email and request for account deletion. Note that active orders, pending
   refunds, or ongoing disputes must be resolved before deletion.

SECTION G: PRODUCTS & AVAILABILITY
=============================================================

Q37: How do I know if a product is in stock?
A: Product availability is shown on the product page. "Add to Cart" means it's
   in stock. "Notify Me" means it's out of stock — enter your email to be
   alerted when it's available again.

Q38: Are the product images accurate?
A: We strive to show accurate product images. However, slight color variations may
   occur due to screen display settings. Product descriptions contain exact
   specifications.

Q39: Can I request a product not listed on ShopNest?
A: Yes. Fill out the "Product Request" form under "Help & Support" and our
   team will try to source it. No guarantees on availability.

Q40: Do ShopNest products come with a manufacturer's warranty?
A: Many electronics, appliances, and branded products come with the manufacturer's
   warranty. Warranty details are listed on individual product pages.

SECTION H: LOYALTY POINTS & OFFERS
=============================================================

Q41: What are ShopNest Rewards Points?
A: You earn 1 Reward Point for every Rs. 100 spent. 100 points = Rs. 10 discount
   on future orders. Points are credited within 24 hours of order delivery.

Q42: Do Reward Points expire?
A: Yes. Points expire 12 months after they are earned if not redeemed.
   Points from cancelled or returned orders are also reversed.

Q43: How do I redeem my Reward Points?
A: At checkout, toggle "Use Reward Points" to apply available points. You can
   use up to 200 points (Rs. 20 discount) per order.

Q44: Why aren't my Reward Points showing up?
A: Points are credited 24 hours after delivery confirmation. If you don't see
   them after 48 hours, contact support@shopnest.com with your order ID.

SECTION I: TECHNICAL ISSUES
=============================================================

Q45: The website is not loading properly. What should I do?
A: Try clearing your browser cache, disabling browser extensions, or switching
   to a different browser or device. If the issue continues, report it at
   support@shopnest.com.

Q46: I'm not receiving OTP on my phone. What should I do?
A: Check that your number is registered correctly. Request OTP again after
   60 seconds. If the issue persists, contact support via email.

Q47: The app keeps crashing. What do I do?
A: Update the ShopNest app to the latest version. Restart your device and
   clear the app cache. If it continues to crash, uninstall and reinstall the app.
