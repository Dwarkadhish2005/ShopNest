REFUND POLICY — ShopNest
Last Updated: April 2026
=============================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
At ShopNest, customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not completely
satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help. This policy outlines the conditions
under which refunds are granted, how to request them, and how they are processed.

---------------------------------------------------------------
1. ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUNDS
---------------------------------------------------------------
You may be eligible for a refund if:

- You request a refund within 30 days of the delivery date.
- The item is unused, unworn, and in its original condition.
- The item is in its original packaging with all tags and accessories included.
- You provide a valid proof of purchase (order confirmation email or order number).

You are NOT eligible for a refund if:
- More than 30 days have passed since the delivery date.
- The item shows signs of use, wear, damage caused by the customer, or missing parts.
- The item is on our Non-Refundable Items list (see Section 5).
- The item was purchased during a final sale or clearance event.

---------------------------------------------------------------
2. TYPES OF REFUNDS
---------------------------------------------------------------
We offer the following types of refunds depending on the situation:

a) Full Refund:
   Issued when the item is returned in original condition within 30 days, or when
   the product received was defective, damaged, or incorrect.

b) Partial Refund:
   Issued when the item is returned with minor signs of use, missing original
   packaging, or after 20-30 days from delivery. A restocking fee of up to 15%
   may be deducted.

c) Store Credit:
   Offered as an alternative to monetary refunds. Store credit does not expire
   and can be used on any future purchase at ShopNest.

d) Exchange:
   If you prefer, we can exchange your item for a different size, color, or product
   of equal value instead of issuing a refund.

---------------------------------------------------------------
3. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND
---------------------------------------------------------------
To initiate a refund, follow these steps:

Step 1: Email us at support@shopnest.com or visit the "My Orders" section on our website.
Step 2: Select the order and the item(s) you want to return.
Step 3: Choose a reason for the return from the dropdown menu.
Step 4: Submit photos of the item if it is defective or damaged.
Step 5: Our team will review your request within 2 business days and send you
        a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and a prepaid return
        shipping label (for eligible cases).
Step 6: Pack the item securely and drop it off at any authorized shipping location.
Step 7: Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed.

---------------------------------------------------------------
4. REFUND PROCESSING TIME
---------------------------------------------------------------
- Refund requests are reviewed within 2 business days of submission.
- Once the returned item is received and inspected, refunds are approved within
  3-5 business days.
- After approval, the refund is credited to your original payment method within
  5-10 business days depending on your bank or card provider.
- Store credits are applied to your ShopNest account instantly upon approval.

Total estimated time from return receipt to refund: 5-15 business days.

---------------------------------------------------------------
5. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS
---------------------------------------------------------------
The following items cannot be refunded or returned:

- Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
- Digital downloads and software licenses
- Gift cards and store credits
- Intimate or sanitary goods (underwear, swimwear) for hygiene reasons
- Hazardous materials or flammable liquids
- Customized or personalized items (engraved, monogrammed, made-to-order)
- Items marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase
- Opened beauty or skincare products (unless defective)

---------------------------------------------------------------
6. RETURN SHIPPING COSTS
---------------------------------------------------------------
- If the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective product, damaged in
  transit): ShopNest covers the return shipping cost with a prepaid label.
- If the return is due to a change of mind or buyer error: The customer is
  responsible for return shipping costs. A flat fee of Rs. 99 (India) / $5 (US)
  will be deducted from the refund amount, or the customer may ship the item
  back at their own cost.
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to
  ShopNest's error.

---------------------------------------------------------------
7. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
---------------------------------------------------------------
If you receive an item that is damaged, defective, or not what you ordered:

- Report the issue within 48 hours of delivery.
- Email support@shopnest.com with your order number and clear photos/videos of
  the damage or defect.
- We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including
  return shipping.

Failure to report damage within 48 hours may affect your eligibility for a
full refund.

---------------------------------------------------------------
8. LATE OR MISSING REFUNDS
---------------------------------------------------------------
If you have not received your refund within the expected timeframe:

1. Check your bank account or card statement again.
2. Contact your credit card company — it may take some time before the refund
   is officially posted.
3. Contact your bank — there is often processing time before a refund shows up.
4. If you have done all of this and still have not received your refund, contact
   us at support@shopnest.com with your order number and RMA number.

---------------------------------------------------------------
9. GIFTS
---------------------------------------------------------------
If an item was marked as a gift at the time of purchase and shipped directly to you:
- You will receive a store credit for the value of the return.
- A gift refund will NOT be sent to the original purchaser.

If the item was not marked as a gift and shipped to the buyer first:
- The refund will be issued to the original purchaser's payment method.
