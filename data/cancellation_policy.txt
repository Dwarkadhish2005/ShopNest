CANCELLATION POLICY — ShopNest
Last Updated: April 2026
=============================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
We understand that plans change. ShopNest allows order cancellations under
certain conditions. This policy explains when you can cancel an order, how to
do it, and what happens after cancellation.

---------------------------------------------------------------
1. CANCELLATION WINDOW
---------------------------------------------------------------
You can cancel your order within the following timeframes:

a) Before Dispatch (Best case — full refund):
   - You may cancel your order any time before it is dispatched from our warehouse.
   - Orders can be cancelled instantly through the "My Orders" section on our website.
   - A full refund is issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

b) After Dispatch but Before Delivery (Limited cancellation):
   - Once an order has been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled directly.
   - You may refuse the delivery at the door. The package will be returned to us.
   - Upon receipt of the returned package, a refund will be initiated minus any
     return shipping charges (Rs. 99 / $5).

c) After Delivery (Treated as a Return):
   - If the order has already been delivered, cancellation is not applicable.
   - Please refer to our Refund Policy for return and refund options.

---------------------------------------------------------------
2. HOW TO CANCEL AN ORDER
---------------------------------------------------------------
Method 1 — Self-Service (Recommended, fastest):
  Step 1: Log into your ShopNest account.
  Step 2: Go to "My Orders" and select the order you wish to cancel.
  Step 3: Click "Cancel Order" and select a reason from the dropdown.
  Step 4: Confirm the cancellation.
  Step 5: You will receive a cancellation confirmation email within minutes.

Method 2 — Contact Support:
  - Email: support@shopnest.com with your Order ID and reason for cancellation.
  - Phone: 1-800-SHOPNEST (Mon-Sat, 9 AM - 6 PM IST)
  - Live Chat: Available on our website (Mon-Sat, 9 AM - 9 PM IST)

Note: Cancellations requested via email or phone are subject to processing time.
If the order is dispatched before we can process your request, it will be
treated as a post-dispatch cancellation.

---------------------------------------------------------------
3. PARTIAL CANCELLATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------
- If your order contains multiple items, you can cancel individual items
  without cancelling the entire order (only if the item has not been dispatched).
- To cancel a specific item, go to "My Orders", select the order, and click
  "Cancel Item" next to the product you want to remove.
- Refund for the cancelled item will be processed within 5-7 business days.

---------------------------------------------------------------
4. CANCELLATION BY SHOPNEST
---------------------------------------------------------------
In rare cases, ShopNest may cancel your order due to the following reasons:

- Item out of stock or discontinued after order placement
- Pricing or product description errors on our website
- Suspected fraudulent activity or policy violations
- Inability to verify payment or customer information
- Shipping restrictions for your delivery address

In such cases:
- We will notify you via email within 24-48 hours.
- A full refund will be issued to your original payment method.
- We will suggest alternative products where possible.

---------------------------------------------------------------
5. CANCELLATION AND REFUND TIMELINE
---------------------------------------------------------------
Cancellation Type           Refund Timing
---------------------------+---------------------------------------
Before dispatch             | Full refund: 5-7 business days
After dispatch (refused)    | Refund after return received: 7-10 business days
                            | Minus return shipping fee (Rs. 99 / $5)
Cancelled by ShopNest       | Full refund: 3-5 business days
Partial cancellation        | Item refund: 5-7 business days

Refunds are credited to:
- Original payment method (credit/debit card, UPI, net banking)
- Wallet balance (if paid via ShopNest wallet)
- Store credit (optional, applied instantly)

---------------------------------------------------------------
6. CANCELLATIONS FOR SPECIAL ORDER TYPES
---------------------------------------------------------------
a) Customized / Personalized Orders:
   - Cannot be cancelled once production has started.
   - Cancellation is only possible within 2 hours of placing the order.
   - Contact us immediately at support@shopnest.com if you need to cancel.

b) Pre-Orders:
   - Pre-orders can be cancelled any time before the item ships.
   - Full refund will be issued to your original payment method.

c) Subscription Orders:
   - You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.
   - Cancelling a subscription stops future billing but does not refund
     already-processed subscription cycles.
   - The subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.

d) Flash Sale / Limited-Time Offers:
   - Orders placed during flash sales can be cancelled only before dispatch.
   - Once dispatched, they follow the post-dispatch cancellation process.
   - Refunds may be issued as store credit in some flash sale scenarios.

---------------------------------------------------------------
7. NON-CANCELLABLE ORDERS
---------------------------------------------------------------
The following orders cannot be cancelled under any circumstances:

- Orders that have already been delivered
- Digital product orders (software, e-books, downloadable content) once
  the download link has been accessed or the product has been activated
- Gift card purchases
- Items that are already in transit and cannot be recalled

---------------------------------------------------------------
8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS — CANCELLATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------
Q: Can I cancel an order on a holiday?
A: Yes. Self-service cancellation via "My Orders" is available 24/7. However,
   support-assisted cancellations are processed on business days only.

Q: What if I accidentally cancel my order?
A: Once an order is cancelled, it cannot be reversed. Please place a new order.
   If the item is back in stock and prices have changed, the new price applies.

Q: Will I be charged a cancellation fee?
A: No. ShopNest does not charge any cancellation fees for orders cancelled
   before dispatch. Post-dispatch cancellations (refused delivery) incur
   a return shipping fee of Rs. 99 / $5.

Q: How will I know my cancellation was successful?
A: You will receive a cancellation confirmation email with details of your
   refund timeline.
